Customer Relationship Management

In OMIS 442 Process and Quality Improvement, I was tasked with analyzing a dataset that required me to find a company that needs improvement in a certain area of their company. I chose to analyzed Method Pharmaceutical's Customer Relationship Management System. In the project, we analyzed the lack of a fundamental system and came to the conclusion that their company would benefit from having a formal fully integrate CRM system.

Kishwakee Hospital Process Improvement

In OMIS 462 Business Systems Analysis Design and Development, I was required to analyze an operation process that needed improvement. I decided to analyze Kishwaukee Hospitals intake process and the time taken to go from first checking in to actually seeing the doctor. In the project, I came to the conclusion that the Hospital would benefit from having a integrated database system that is stores patient information such as allergies, medication, and appointments.

Google Play Store Statistical Analysis

In OMIS 324 Introduction to Data Analytics, I was asked to find any dataset I wanted and analyze it. I decided to analyze the Google Play Store. I wanted to find out if it was worth paying for an application for the add features or downloading a free application. In the project, I ran a regression analysis to see if there were any correlations between variables and made multiple visuals like a bar chart, scatter plot, and heat map.
