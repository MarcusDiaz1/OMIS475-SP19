Sports

In my free time I love to play different sports, depending which season it is. During the summer time I play baseball in a men’s recreational summer league called the Latina American League and I also like to golf on the weekends when I have time. During the winter I participate in a men’s recreational basketball league and I also bowl in a Friday night league with my brothers and dad. I stay very active playing sports because it is a great way to relieve stress and it helps keep my body active.

Traveling

Whenever I get the chance, I love to travel anywhere I get the opportunity. Within the last year I have traveled around the US and got to see different aspects of America that I am not accustomed to seeing. Traveling is a great way to get the full experiences of life while all learning about yourself. In the future, I plan on traveling outside of the US so that I can experience different cultures that I cannot see here in America.
