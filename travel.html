Colorado

I visited Colorado in the summer of 2016 and it was one of the best trips of my life. I loved he constant adventures as we drove and hiked up Colorado’s beautiful mountains. I stayed in Golden and visited Denver, the Red Rocks, and Colorado Springs while on my visit. The weather was perfect, and everyone was super friendly. I would highly recommend visiting Colorado and the Mile-High City.

Florida

I have been to Florida multiple times in my life. My parents have friends that live in Naples, Florida so I have visited them several times over the years. I have explored majority of the state either through baseball tournaments or while on family vacations. My most recent visit was last year when I went to Miami, Fort Lauderdale, and Naples on a little get away after school finished.

Niagara Falls

I visited Niagara Falls in the summer of 2017. During my trip I stayed in Buffalo New York and had some of the best wings of my life. Buffalo is where the wings originated from so I had to try all the recommended restaurants like Anchor Bar. Niagara’s giant waterfalls and monstrous rapids are a must-see phenomenon and will always be an unforgettable trip for myself.
